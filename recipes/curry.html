Japanese curry

Description

Curry with rice is a staple dish of Japan, enjoyed by natives and foreigners alike. There are many variations to this dish and you are able to substitute any main vegetable or meat of this dish with any vegetable or meat of your liking. This dish is very hearty and delicious and perfect to be stored for leftovers!

Ingredients

  • Beef 2lbs
  • Potatoes 10oz
  • Carrots 10oz
  • Onions 2lbs
  • Curry seasoning packet 1 box
  • Water 5 cups
  • Vegetable oil 3 tbsp

Steps

  1. Cut beef into bite-sized pieces, and slice other vegetables to the size of your liking.
  2. Add beef and oil to pot and cook until brown.
  3. Add all other vegetables to the pot and stir fry for 5 minutes.
  4. Add water to pot, bring to boil, set heat to low and put cover over pot to let simmer.
  5. Simmer for about 15 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
  6. Turn off heat, break and add sauce packet while stirring unitl fully mixed and serve over rice.
