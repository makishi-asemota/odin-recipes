Stir Fry

Description

Stir-fry is a very versatile, customizable dish that is very simple and easy to learn! With just a few simple steps, you are going to be able to master stir-fry and make it a staple in any dish you serve with it or by itself. The only requirement is that you have a good wok or frying pan because it is essential in the process of making stir-fry!

Ingredients

  • General Tso's sauce 6oz
  • Onion 10oz
  • Vegetable Oil 4 tbsp
  • Chicken/Beef/Pork/other 10oz
  • Green or Red pepper 10oz
  • Broccoli 12oz
  • Rice 2cups
  • Eggs 3

Steps

  1. Boil rice. Chop Onions, pepper, and meat to the size of your liking.
  2. Set heat to medium and add in oil. First, stir fry meat until browned. Boil broccoli and set aside.
  3. Add in all vegetables and stir fry until they are tender. Approx. 7min
  4. Add in rice and stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  5. Push rice/vegetables to one side of the pan and add in one more tbsp of oil, crack, and scramble eggs.
  6. Bring rice and eggs together and add in sauce. stir-fry for an additional minute.
  7. Bring off heat, and enjoy!
